Holiday Hours


In 2026, the Library will be closed:

  • New Year’s Day: Thursday, Jan. 1
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Day: Monday, Jan. 19
  • Presidents Day: Monday, Feb. 16
  • Patriots’ Day: Monday, April 20
  • Memorial Day: Monday, May 25
  • Staff Development Day: Friday, June 5
  • Juneteenth: Friday, June 19
  • Independence Day: Friday, July 3 and Saturday, July 4
  • Labor Day: Sunday, Sept. 6 and Monday, Sept. 7
  • Indigenous Peoples Day: Monday, Oct. 12
  • Veterans Day: Wednesday, Nov. 11
  • Day Before Thanksgiving: Wednesday, Nov. 25 - closing at 5 p.m.
  • Thanksgiving Day: Thursday, Nov. 26
  • Day After Thanksgiving: Friday, Nov. 27
  • Christmas Eve: Thursday, Dec. 24
  • Christmas: Friday, Dec. 25
  • New Year's Eve: Thursday, Dec. 31 - closing at 5 p.m.
