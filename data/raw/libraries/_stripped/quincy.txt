Library Accounts

Get a Thomas Crane Public Library card! Sign up at any TCPL location or online.

Library cards are available for free! Massachusetts residents can apply for an e-card online. With an e-card, you can access and use our online resources and databases, borrow eBooks and eAudiobooks, and stream music, movies, and TV online.

A permanent card is required for the borrowing of physical materials. To register for a permanent card, to upgrade your e-card to a permanent card, or to get a replacement card, visit any TCPL location and bring proof of identification.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What do I need for proof of identification?

A photo ID with a current home address or a photo ID along with documentation that contains both your name and current address.

Examples of valid photo identification include:

Drivers license
State or federal identification
Passport
Student ID card
Green card
Military identification
If your photo identification does not have your current address, then please also show one of the following:

A delivered piece of mail to your current address
Printed checks with your current address on them
Paycheck or stub with your address on it
Lease/housing agreement with your current address on it

How do I renew items?

Most TCPL items will be automatically renewed three times, with the exception of items that are on hold for other library users. Items from other Old Colony Library Network libraries will be automatically renewed twice.

You can also manually renew items online up to three days before the due date. The renewal period will begin as of the due date.

Go to the catalog, click on My Account , and sign in with your library barcode number and PIN.
Click on Checkouts tab.
Select the items you would like to renew by clicking on the small square next to the item(s) and click Renew .
If items have been successfully renewed, you will see a message with the new due date.

How do I place a hold?

You can place a hold on library items in person, on the phone, or online with your library card. To place a hold online:

Go to the catalog, click My Account and enter your library barcode number and PIN.
Search for items by Title, Author, Subject, etc.
Once you have identified the item you wish to place on hold, click the Place Hold button to the right of the item.
Select a location to pick up the item and then click Place Hold(s).
You will see a confirmation screen and then be returned to your catalog search results.

How can I view my holds?

You can see a list of your holds and available items by logging into your library account.

Go to the catalog, click on My Account and enter your library barcode number and PIN.
Click the Holds tab (near the top center of the screen).
You will see a list of Items on Hold, which will include items not yet available for pickup (pending) or those that are ready for pickup (available).
From this screen, you may also modify or cancel your holds.

How do I pay for overdue fees?

TCPL does not charge overdue fees for any Library-owned items! However, museum passes will accrue an automatic $20 overdue fee if returned late. You may also accrue overdue fees on items owned by other Old Colony Library Network libraries, according to the policies of the owning library.

You can pay overdue fees in person at any of our locations, or at any other Old Colony Library Network member library. Library staff can only accept cash and checks made out to Thomas Crane Public Library.

For amounts of $5 or more, you can also pay online using a credit card. Online payment is immediate. If you have been blocked from placing holds or renewing items because of outstanding charges, paying online will immediately resolve your account charges.

Log into your library account .
Select Fines/Current Fines and follow the on-screen prompts.
