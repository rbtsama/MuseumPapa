What's New at the Library

High Tide, Low Tide: Me, My Stuff, and I

Join us on Saturday, May 30 at 10:00 for a lively discussion with Lee Shuer, a hoarding disorder expert with lived experience, who understands the challenges of reducing clutter and offers practical, supportive approaches to help. Learn more and register...

Sound Bath Experience with Claudia D'Emilia Iafrate

Relax and de-stress with a Sound Bath workshop led by Claudia D'Emilia Iafrate on Tuesday, June 2 at 7:00. Space is limited, please register. Learn more and register...

Junior Gardeners with the Hingham Garden Club

Learn about the beauty of nature on Tuesday, June 2 at 4:00. Junior Gardeners make seasonal crafts that center around plants. Expect to get your hands dirty! This program is for students in grades 3-5. Learn more and register...

The Queer Language of Flowers

In honor of Pride Month, join us for a presentation on queer history, identity, and self-expression through floriography, or, the secret language of flowers. Wednesday, June 3 at 7:00 on Zoom. Register for the link. Learn more and register for the zoom link...

Intro to Apple Photos

This two-part class (Thursdays, June 4 & 11 at 10:00) focuses on managing, sharing, and editing photos on your iPhone or iPad. Bring your devices and questions! Learn more and register...

HPL Movie: "Solo Mio"

When a wedding disaster leaves a groom stranded in Rome, his ruined honeymoon becomes an adventure he never expected. Showing twice: Wednesday, June 10 at 7:00 & Thursday, June 11 at 1:00. Learn more...

Important LGBTQ Artists

Join artist Suzanne Altman for a look at the history and legacy of notable LGBTQ artists. Monday, June 8 at 6:30 on Zoom. Learn more and register for the zoom link...
