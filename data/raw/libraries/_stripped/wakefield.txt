Get a Library Card

Anyone is eligible for a library card with valid identification and proof of current address (e.g. a driver’s license and utility bill).

Register for a library card from your computer or smartphone to access digital content right away. Sign up for a NOBLE eCard and once your address is verified, you will receive an email with your library card number to instantly access ebooks, audiobooks, magazines, and more.

The eCard is for patrons who do not already have a NOBLE public library card and may be upgraded to a physical library card with borrowing privileges by visiting the library.

Not a Wakefield Resident?

Valid cards from other NOBLE libraries are accepted in Wakefield. Beebe Library does not extend borrowing or other privileges to residents of communities with uncertified libraries. See our policy on “Service to Uncertified Libraries and Their Patrons” for further information.

Children

Children of any age can apply for their own library card. A parent or guardian must sign for any child under the age of 16.
