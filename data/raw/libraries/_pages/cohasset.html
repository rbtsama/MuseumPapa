  2. Stay Up To Date

  1. Relax in our new Reading Nooks!

    We are delighted to share that through the “Making It Public for MAP Municipalities” grant, the Paul Pratt Memorial Library has received two reading nooks by artist Kim Bernard! Additional Info...

  2. Drop-In Social Worker Hours

    Are you in need of support or are looking for guidance? The Town of Cohasset offers social work services to residents as well as drop-in hours at the library. Learn more about this amazing resource. Additional Info...

  3. Summer Reading is Coming!

    "PLANT A SEED, READ!" this summer during the Statewide Summer Library Program! Learn more about what's kicking off at the PPML on Monday, June 22nd. Learn More...

  4. New! PPML Seed Library

    We invite you to explore our diverse selection of seeds and see what you can cultivate throughout the year! Click to learn more.

  5. Access to Justice Program

    The Paul Pratt Memorial Library is proud to partner with the Massachusetts Trial Court System to offer virtual court access through the Access to Justice program. Click to learn more.

  6. Podcasts for Kids

    Embark on the next exciting journey with podcasts designed for children. These engaging audio experiences cover a wide range of topics, including science, history, stories, music, and much more!

  7. PPML Home Delivery Service

    Having trouble getting to the PPML? Let us bring the PPML to you! Our dedicated staff is more than happy to come to your home to fulfill your library material requests. Learn more at the link below! Additional Info...

  8. Pavilion Construction Beginning 8/29

    Beginning Friday, August 29th, patrons may observe fencing, demolition, and construction activities taking place on Sohier Street.
