Get a Library Card

Any Boxford Resident is eligible to obtain a Boxford Town Library Account and Card. To obtain an account please follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Come to the Library located at 7A Spofford Road, Boxford. MA
  2. Bring a form of Identification (See Below) and Proof of Residency (See below)
  3. Fill out the Library Account and Card application

Currently, accounts are created in person. If you are unable to come in for a visit please call the library at 978-887-7323 to see how we can help you get set up with a library account and card.

Acceptable form of Positive Identification (Must include photo and full name)

  • Identification issued by the United States Department of State
    • United States Passport (Current or expired)
    • United States Passport Identification Card (Current or expired)
  • Identification issued by the United States Citizenship and Immigration Service
    • Green Card
    • Identification issued by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts
  • Massachusetts Driver's License
    • Massachusetts Identification Card
    • Other State issued identification cards (i.e. NH Driver's License)

Acceptable forms of Proof of Residency (Must include Boxford address and full name that matches the form of Positive Identification)

  • Identification issued by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts (may serve as both Positive ID and Proof of Residency if the current address on the card is in Boxford)
    • Massachusetts Driver's License
    • Massachusetts Identification Card
  • Official documents issued by the Town of Boxford including but not exclusive to:
    • Tax bills
      • Excise Tax
      • Property Tax
  • Bank Records including but not exclusive to:
    • Bank Statement
    • Personal Check
    • Mortgage Statement and or closing documents
  • Utility Statements including but not exclusive to:
    • Cable Television (i.e. Comcast, Verizon, etc.)
    • Electricity (i.e. National Grid)
    • Gas Company (I.e. National Grid)
    • Cellular Phone (i.e. Verizon, AT&T, Sprint, etc.)
