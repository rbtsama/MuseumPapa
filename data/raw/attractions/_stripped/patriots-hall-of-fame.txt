Admission/Hours
The Hall is open seven days a week (daily) from 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM*.
Our hours of operation are subject to change so please feel free to confirm by checking back on the website or by calling (508) 698-4800.
*Please note that the last ticket is sold 30 minutes before closing, and any tickets requiring ID must be purchased in person.
Upcoming Closures (as of May 8, 2026):
Due to the FIFA World Cup event, the Patriots Hall of Fame will be closed beginning May 14, 2026 through the conclusion of the event.
The Patriots Hall of Fame presented by RTX is closed on Thanksgiving, Christmas and Easter . It also closes for private events. Those closures will be listed here or can be confirmed by calling (508) 698-4800.
The Hall is Cashless! The Patriots Hall of Fame is now completely cashless. Guests can utilize our Cash-to-Card kiosk, located next to the registers in the ProShop, to convert cash to Visa® prepaid cards. Visa® prepaid cards will be valid anywhere Visa® is accepted. There is no cost to get a card, and there are no fees to use the card. Cards can be used anywhere, inside or outside of the stadium. Any money remaining on a card after an event can be spent outside of the stadium. Cards are not reloadable. The Hall accepts Visa, Mastercard, and American Express; we do not accept Discover.

Directions and Parking

Parking
Parking at The Patriots Hall of Fame presented by RTX is free, except for ticketed event days when parking rates apply. To park closest to The Hall, enter Patriot Place at the P1 entrance and take the first right toward the Hall and Showcase Cinema.
School Bus Parking
School busses can drop students off as close to The Hall as possible adjacent to the parking lot nearest the ProShop and The Hall. Once students have cleared the area, busses can be parked anywhere in Parking Lot 4.

Book a Function
Please note that the information below is general function information. Please call us at (508) 698-4800 for more information or call the number below to book a function.
The Patriots Hall of Fame presented by RTX is the most distinctive event space at what is New England’s most unique event venue. Gillette Stadium and The Hall are an extension of the team’s effort to strive for on-field excellence.
If you are hoping to impress high-powered corporate clients with the professionalism and functionality of your services or want to break from the norm of holding your banquet or reception in a hotel ballroom, then you should reach out to our event planners at Gillette Stadium.
No matter the reason, we want to make your ideas a reality. The Patriots Hall of Fame presented by RTX has a large Grand Hall that serves as the main function space within its 20,000 square feet of interactive exhibits spread over two floors.
The Hall features the 150-seat RTX Theater, which is home to our inspiring, panoramic 17-minute signature film, titled Patriots Way . The theater also is equipped with a dedicated projector and sound system for use with presentations.
To book your next event at The Hall or inquire about doing so, call ( 508) 384-4378 or click here for more information.

Book a Group
The Patriots Hall of Fame presented by RTX entertains groups of all kinds from corporate outings to school field trips to summer camps to birthday parties and more. We are happy to answer your questions regarding group visits. Please either call (508) 549-0555 or email us at [email protected] for more information.
The Hall is a one-of-a-kind experience your group is certain to enjoy. We offer traditional museum artifacts combined with cutting-edge technology and interactivity perfect for all ages.
The Hall offers a 30% discount off regular adult admission ($7) for groups of 15 or more. To book your group reservation and ask about our group rates, email [email protected] . You will need a 50 percent non-refundable deposit to secure your reservation. We prefer to have final payment two weeks before your visit.
School Field Trips – Booking a Field Trip For more information visit our Field Trip Page .
Camps and Children’s Groups When booking a camp or children’s group of 15 or more, the children ages 5-12 will receive the regular $5 admission rate while any group members 12 or older will receive the discounted $7 adult group rate. To book your group reservation and ask about our group rates, please call ( 508) 549-0555 or email [email protected] .
Birthdays The Patriots Hall of Fame presented by RTX does not offer space to host birthday parties, but we do encourage you to bring your group to The Hall as part of your birthday celebration. We still want to celebrate with you. The child celebrating a birthday will receive a goody bag including a special birthday certificate from The Hall to recognize the child’s special day. Please provide 5 days notice so that we can process your request. Email [email protected] with the subject BIRTHDAY for more information and to let us know when your group will be visiting.
