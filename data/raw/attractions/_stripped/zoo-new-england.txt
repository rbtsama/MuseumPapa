Buy Tickets

Saturday, June 6: Franklin Park Zoo will be closed in preparation for our Zootopia gala . Thank you for your understanding, and we look forward to welcoming you on another day.

• Tickets are released intermittently and may not be available more than two weeks in advance. • Last entry is one hour before closing. • Can't find your ticket? Look up your order or visit our Ticket FAQs below .

Buy Franklin Park Zoo Tickets

With Plan Ahead Pricing, pick the day and time that works best for your budget. The further in advance you purchase, the more you save! Tickets may also be purchased at our gates.

Boston Family Days

On the first and second Sunday of each month through December 2026, Franklin Park Zoo offers free admission for every student living in Boston enrolled in grades K-12 or Boston Pre-K and two of their family members.

Registration, Tickets & Full Details

ConnectorCare Tickets

ConnectorCare cardholders receive general admission for $10 per person for up to four people at Franklin Park Zoo. Offer is valid for Massachusetts residents through Dec. 31, 2026.

Online Tickets: Use promo code CARE when purchasing tickets to receive this admission price. Please be ready to present your card at the Admissions booth upon arrival.

Gate Tickets: Please be ready to present your card at the Admissions booth upon arrival.

If you have any questions, please call 617-989-2076 and our staff will be happy to assist you.

EBT & WIC Tickets

Massachusetts EBT and WIC cardholders receive general daytime admission for $5 per person, for up to four people. Offer is valid for Massachusetts residents through Dec. 31, 2026.

Online Tickets (EBT only): To receive the EBT discount for online purchases, you need to verify your eligibility prior to purchasing your tickets. You will be prompted to log into or create an account with ID.Me to verify your identity and EBT affiliation. For help troubleshooting the Id.me application, please visit the ID.me Help Center . Please note: ID.me cannot be used for WIC accounts. WIC tickets must be purchased at the gate.

Purchase Discounted EBT Tickets

Gate Tickets: If purchasing tickets at the gate, please be ready to present your card at the Admissions booth upon arrival.

Please call 617-989-2076 with any questions, and our staff will be happy to assist you.

Group Rates

We offer admission discounts for groups of 10 or more non-members. Full Details

Library Passes

To use the library discount, you must present the physical library ticket at the Zoo's admission booth. Reservations are not required for this discount. Please note: we will not accept any confirmation emails; you must have the physical ticket. Check with your library about when to pick up the pass, as many libraries are closed on weekends. Library passes are good for daytime admission only.

Contact us for more information.

Military/Teacher/Reciprocal Discounts

Military:

All active military personnel and their dependents receive a 50% discount on general, daytime admission. Active military personnel also receive discounts on membership .

Purchase Discounted Tickets for Military

To receive the military discount for online purchases, you will need to verify your eligibility prior to purchasing your tickets. You will be prompted to log into or create an account with ID.Me to verify your identity and affiliation. For help troubleshooting the Id.me application, please visit the ID.me Help Center .

Teachers:

Teachers who are part of MTA, BTU, or AFT- MA chapter receive a 50% discount on general, daytime admission. Teachers also receive discounts on membership . Please be ready to present the appropriate ID for each individual. Offer available at admissions gate for in-person rates only, not online tickets.

Reciprocal Memberships:

Members of many AZA accredited institutions receive a 50% discount on general, daytime admission.

Terms and Conditions

A valid ticket is required for Zoo entrance for each visitor. There is no re-entry to the Zoo after your ticket has been scanned.

Tickets are:

One-time-use only and valid for the date and time noted.

Only valid at the location indicated at time of purchase.

Non-refundable, have no cash value, cannot be used for private or after-hours events and cannot be combined with any other offers.

Rain or shine. In the rare occasion that the Zoo closes due to inclement weather, ticket holders will be notified and refunded.

Ticket FAQs

How do I retrieve my ticket?

Tickets purchased online are delivered to the email address provided (please allow up to one hour for receipt). Please note, your emailed receipt is not your ticket. You will need to download your barcoded tickets from your email and present them at the Admissions booth.

You can also use the "order lookup" function in the menu of our ticketing site for access to your tickets. You will need to provide the email address and phone number used during purchase.

How does online pricing work?

With Plan-Ahead Pricing, you can pick the date and time that works best with your schedule and budget. The further in advance you purchase tickets, the more you’re likely to save!

Children under age two are free.

A $1.50 processing fee applies per ticket .

After you click the "Buy Tickets" button above, you'll see available dates/times and the respective ticket prices. Each day’s prices are determined by a number of factors, including attendance patterns, popular holidays/events, day of the week and weather.
