Family owned for over 35 years.

Since its creation in 1990, the Charles River Boat Company has offered guests the chance to experience beautiful views of both the Charles River and Boston Harbor. Charles River Boat Company is proud to showcase the incredible skyline to over 60,0000 passengers every season and is built on the ideals of genuine customer service, community engagement, and making memories with those who matter most.

New England Seafood Experiences
Celebrate on the water in true New England style with a private seafood bake or raw bar cruise! Enjoy lobster bakes featuring local shellfish, chowder, and all the fixings as you take in the beautiful views of the Boston skyline. About Us

Since its creation in 1990, the Charles River Boat Company has offered guests the chance to experience the sights of Boston from the best vantage point in the city, while highlighting Boston’s rich maritime history. We are the only company offering cruises on the historic Charles River with views of both Boston and Cambridge.
A family run business, the Charles River Boat Company was founded with the ideals of providing a comfortable environment, genuine guides, and an alternative approach to exploring the great city of Boston – for locals and visitors alike. Starting with just one boat, the Charles River Boat Company has grown to a fleet of 8 vessels carrying over 80,000 passengers per year!

With spectacular views of the Boston skyline, Old Ironsides, Harvard University and more, our sightseeing tours and private cruises are nothing less than spectacular. Whether you are planning an intimate group event or looking for an enjoyable afternoon adventure, come cruise with us and experience the relaxing and entertaining atmosphere on the Charles River and Boston Harbor!
Please click here for directions to our docking locations . Please feel free to contact us or call us at 617-621-3001 with any questions. We look forward to having you onboard!

Our Memberships

With over 30 years of service, we deeply appreciate our community’s support and are committed to being the most environmentally and socially responsible boat company in New England. We support local community, educational, and non-profit organizations by donating over $1.5 million in cruise tickets, charters, and cash contributions. Our mission focuses on sustainability, education, and community outreach. We invest in cleaning local waterways, offer educational tours, and collaborate with schools and youth programs. Additionally, we donate generously to non-profits, including Make-A-Wish Foundation and cancer organizations, fulfilling thousands of donation requests annually.

Directions

Below are the directions to our main two docking locations; the CambridgeSide Mall and Rowes Wharf. Our boats dock at multiple locations, so please be sure to note your cruise information and follow all boarding directions. If you have any questions regarding the boarding location, please call our sales office at 617-621-3001 .

CambridgeSide
Rowes Wharf
Parking Information

CambridgeSide

CambridgeSide

Our boats are in the Lechmere Canal, right outside the CanalSide Food Hall.
100 Cambridgeside Pl,
Cambridge, MA 02141

Get Directions →

From North of Boston

Take Interstate 93 exit 26 for Storrow Drive / North Station Keep right at the fork, follow signs for 28 North / Leverett Circle / North Station Slight right at Nashua St Turn left onto Nashua St Take the 1st right onto MA-28 North / Monsignor O’Brien Highway At the next light turn left onto Edwin H Land Boulevard At the next light turn right onto CambridgeSide Place Park in the CambridgeSide Mall Parking Garage. Our boats are located outside the CanalSide Food Hall. Please note we are on the opposite side of the mall from the Cheesecake Factory.

From South of Boston

Take Interstate 93 exit 26 toward Storrow Drive / North Station Keep left at the fork, follow signs for MA-28 North / Leverett Circle / North Station Slight right at Nashua St Turn left onto Nashua St Take the 1st right onto MA-28 North / Monsignor O’Brien Highway At the next light turn left onto Edwin H Land Boulevard At the next light turn right onto CambridgeSide Place Park in the CambridgeSide Mall Parking Garage. Our boats are located outside the CanalSide Food Hall. Please note we are on the opposite side of the mall from the Cheesecake Factory.

From West of Boston

Take Route 90 – Mass Pike Exit Left at Cambridge/Allston Tolls – follow signs to Cambridge Proceed through lights and over River Street Bridge (Charles River) Immediately turn right at other end of bridge and travel East on Memorial Drive Stay on Memorial Drive – The CambridgeSide mall is approximately 3 miles on the left across from the Sonesta Hotel
Park in the CambridgeSide Mall Parking Garage. Our boats are located outside the CanalSide Food Hall. Please note we are on the opposite side of the mall from the Cheesecake Factory.

From The MBTA (Public Transit)

Take the Green Line “E” train to Lechmere Station Exit the train, cross McGrath Highway and Cambridge St. After crossing Cambridge St., walk down First St. Take a left at Canal Park and this will take you into the Lechmere Canal Follow the footpath to the right, the docks are located outside the CanalSide Food Hall.

Rowes Wharf

Rowes Wharf – Gate A & C

Rowes Wharf 70 Rowes Wharf Boston, MA 02110

Charles River Boat Company offers dockage at Gate A and Gate C. Please check your event-specific directions for your gate assignment.

Get Directions →

From North of Boston

Take Route 93 South to Exit 23 – Purchase street At the top of the ramp, take a left at the light then immediately at the next light turn left again onto Atlantic Ave (James Hook & Co. on right side.) Rowes Wharf and The Boston Harbor Hotel will be on right hand side. There is garage parking at the hotel and several other garages on both sides of Atlantic Ave.
The vessels are located at Rowes Wharf at either Gate A or Gate C.

From South of Boston

Take Route 93 North to Boston – Take Government Center Exit Bear left at top of ramp and turn left on Surface Road Proceed down Surface Road and turn left on Congress Street Immediately take another left onto Atlantic Ave and proceed through second set of lights, Rowes Wharf is on the right side . There is garage parking at the Boston Harbor Hotel and several other garages on both sides of Atlantic Ave.
The vessels are located at Rowes Wharf at either Gate A or Gate C.

From West of Boston

Follow the Massachusetts Turnpike/Interstate 90 East to Exit 25 – South Boston At the second set of lights, take a left onto Seaport Boulevard. Proceed west on Seaport Boulevard over the Moakley Bridge and turn right on Atlantic Ave. (James Hook Lobster on right.) Rowes Wharf and The Boston Harbor Hotel will be on the right hand side. There is garage parking at the hotel and several other garages on both sides of Atlantic Ave.
The vessels are located at Rowes Wharf at either Gate A or Gate C.

From The MBTA (Public Transit)

Take the Red Line to South Station Walk north on Atlantic Ave and Rowes Wharf will be on the right hand side. The vessels are located at Gate A directly underneath the archway.
The vessels are located at Rowes Wharf at either Gate A or Gate C.

Parking Information
CambridgeSide – Cambridge Parking

Charles River Boat Company is proud to offer our customers discounted parking at a rate of $4.00 for up to 8 hours at the CambridgeSide Mall parking garage. Guests may park at the main garage at 100 CambridgeSide Place, Cambridge, MA 02141. Once you enter the garage, go straight; do not turn left into the Royal Sonesta parking. Parking kiosks are located on each floor of the mall before entering the garage. Please pay for parking before returning to your vehicle.
Garage Information: $4 for 8 hours of parking, with validation. Please ask our on-site staff for a parking validation chaser ticket. Must park in the CambridgeSide Mall parking garage. We cannot validate parking for the Royal Sonesta parking garage.

Rowes Wharf – Boston’s Financial District Parking

Parking can be found in the Rowes Wharf Garage located at 30 Rowes Wharf, Boston, MA 02110. Please visit their website for parking rates. Garage parking and valet service are available at the Boston Harbor Hotel. There are also options for street parking and other garages in the area.

July 4th USS Constitution Turn-Around Cruise

Date: July 4th

Join us to witness the USS Constitution’s annual turnaround sail on the Fourth of July. This daytime Turnaround is a celebration of the nation’s birthday and involves the ship sailing out into Boston Harbor, firing a 21-gun salute, and then returning to the inner harbor.
