Friendly Reminders
Know Before You Go

No Food or Beverage
No food & beverage (except water) allowed inside of the museum. All food and beverage must be consumed on the public concourse or in one of the many on-site restaurants.

No Backpacks or Basketballs
Backpacks, any large bags, & basketballs are not allowed inside of the museum, please leave them in your vehicle.

Sneakers Recommended
For your safety, sneakers are recommended while shooting baskets on Center Court. Sandals & slides are not allowed in the Slam Dunk area.

No Games on Court
For your safety, no games of any kind or half court shots on Center Court.

Discover the birthplace of basketball

The Naismith Basketball Hall of Fame is a fantastic destination for everyone from lifelong basketball fans to families with children who are just beginning to explore the world's most popular sport. The Hall of Fame is located in "The Birthplace of Basketball", beautiful Springfield, Massachusetts. The city is easily accessible no matter what direction you are traveling from.

Ticket Pricing

Adults

$34

Youth (5-12)

$20

Seniors (65+)

$25

Prices may vary based on ticket type.

Museum Hours

10am - 4pm

Open Daily

The Hall of Fame may be closed certain holidays. Click below for special hours.

Hours of operation are subject to change. The Hall of Fame hosts many special events, which can close parts of the Museum.
