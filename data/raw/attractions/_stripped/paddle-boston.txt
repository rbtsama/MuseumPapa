Directions:

From the North or South on Rt. 128

Take Exit 22 and stay right on Grove Street. Follow Grove street and continue onto Lexington Street. Turn left onto Rumford Avenue and Left onto Riverview Avenue. We are on the right.
From the West

Take the MassPike to Exit 14 (Rt. 128/I-95/Rt. 30). Stay left through the toll (follow signs for I-95 North) and before reaching the highway take Exit 24 for Rt. 30. Turn right onto Rt. 30 (Commonwealth Ave) East and left onto Lexington Street. Turn left onto Rumford Avenue and left onto Riverview Avenue. We are on the right.
From the East (including Boston)

Take the MassPike to Exit 15 (Rt. 128/I-95/Rt. 30). Stay right through the toll (follow signs for I-95 North) and before reaching the highway take Exit 24 for Rt. 30. Turn right onto Rt. 30 (Commonwealth Ave) East and left onto Lexington Street. Turn left onto Rumford Avenue and left onto Riverview Avenue. We are on the right.
By Commuter Rail

Take the MBTA Commuter Rail's Fitchburg Line to Waltham. Turn left on Moody Street and Right on Crescent Street. After 0.7 miles turn right on Woerd Avenue and left onto Riverview. The distance is approximately 1.2 miles and usually takes about 25 minutes to walk.

Ratios For Group Outings

Group Rates & Reservations

Reservations are available for groups renting eight boats or more , or for events with guiding/instruction. If you are renting fewer than eight boats, just come down — boats come off the water as quickly as they are going out, so it’s rare that you are waiting more than 30 minutes and often less at our quieter sites.

All reservations require pre-payment. Examine our policy below, get an Instant Quote for your group, and please call (617) 965-5110 or e-mail us if you have questions.

Kids’ groups will need a sufficient number of adults to accompany their group on the water. See our maximum child-to-adult ratios for details.

Special Rates for School Groups (K-12) and Camps

When renting 8-9 boats: 20% off regular rates.

When renting 10-14 boats: 40% off regular rates.

When renting 15 boats or more: 50% off regular rates.

No discounts are offered during our peak hours of 11am to 5pm on Weekends and Holidays.

Customized instruction is available for $105/hour or $380/day per instructor.

For unguided groups see our child-to-adult ratios for details.

Don’t let cost be a deterrent for your group! For people with disabilities or anyone who needs help getting out on the water, email us at [email protected] to learn about our grant program.

Reservations Are Required for Groups Get a Quote — Request a Date !
Registration/Cancellation Policy for Paddling Group Outings
Reservations cancelled by the following deadlines receive either a refund of the pre-payment less a 10% registration fee, or a full credit to a store account. This credit is valid for 7 years and may be used for instruction, rentals, or purchases at Charles River Canoe & Kayak or Weston Ski Track.
Unguided Group Reservations at our locations must be cancelled 48 hours in advance. Cancellations due to weather must be made at least 2 hours before the event.
Group Reservations with instructors or guides must be cancelled at least 7 days before the event.
Boat Deliveries must be cancelled at least 48 hours prior to the event. Cancellations due to weather must be made by 5pm the night before the event.
Off-Site Guiding/Instruction must be cancelled at least 7 days before the event. Cancellations due to weather must be made by 5pm the night before the event.
For all cancellations made after the above deadlines, no refunds or credits will be issued .

1

Seven Rental Locations

All locations are on the water and offer canoes, kayaks, and paddleboards. Explore all our rental locations and you'll find a wide range of paddling experiences and environments!

2

Boston: Allston/Brighton

Our Boston location offers the best of both worlds: plenty of nature with parkland on both shores, then paddle towards the city and take in the skyline. You can also paddle one way and end your trip out our Kendall location, nearby plenty of restaurants, bars, and public transit... [more]

3

Cambridge: Kendall Square

Paddle in the heart of Boston and Cambridge from this location. Just a short walk from the Red line, You can paddle through the Esplanade and get phenomenal views of the skyline, the Zakim Bridge, and the Museum of Science... [more]

4

Medford: Condon Shell

Visit Paddle Boston's newest location on the Mystic River. Enjoy this seven-mile stretch of river, steps from Medford Square, the Condon Shell Farmers Market, and a scenic two-mile paddle to the Shannon Beach picnic and swimming area.... [more]

5

Newton: Historic Boathouse

Visit the Historic Newton Boathouse and enjoy the six-mile stretch of calm water and scenic tree and park lined shores. Stop for a picnic at Forest Grove and be on the lookout for native great blue herons, egrets, and other wildlife.... [more]

6

Somerville: Blessing of the Bay Boathouse

Come visit our newest location on the Mystic River! You can paddle the full seven miles of the Mystic River, including skyline views and access to the scenic lakes and swimming area. We're a short one mile walk from the Assembly Row train station, shops, and bars... [more]

8

Waltham: Moody Street Dam

Our Waltham location covers a six mile stretch of the Charles River known as the lakes district. This section, largely bordered by parkland and forest, was once a haven for canoeists looking to escape the city — and is again today! End your paddle with ice cream or dinner at one of the many restaurants and bars on Moody Street.... [more]
