What Our Members Say

The Y has such amazing programming for children. Both of our kids have attended the Preschool which has done such a great job preparing them for school, and we’ve also done the swim lessons once our daughter started going to elementary school (weekly swim lessons are included for the preschool!! One of the greatest perks!). All of the staff have been so warm and welcoming and I cannot say enough great things about them! Also wanted to provide a special call out for how the Y handled the recent NTA strike - they quickly pivoted and provided a much needed program for working parents of Newton. My daughter came home every day with new crafts and stories of her new friends!

— Katie Murphy

Great YMCA - surprisingly large on the inside, multiple pools, rooms for yoga and fitness classes, a basketball gym, full workout room with all the machines and free weights you could need. Good atmosphere.

Racquetball Court

Located at the Church St Y

Challenge yourself to a thrilling game of racquetball at the West Suburban YMCA! Whether you prefer singles or doubles, grab a friend (or three) and head over to the Church St Y for an exhilarating workout while having a blast.
Make A Reservation for Church St Y Racquetball Court
Reservations can be made no more than 48 hours in advance. Use this tool to reserve an appointment for use of the Church St Y squash/racquetball court.
Appointment times will not appear in the search until you are within 48 hours of the start time.
Please log in with your full first and last name, along with the birth date, email and phone number that is listed on our member software.

Join Our Team

If you want more than just a job—if you’re looking to discover a sense of purpose and opportunities to contribute to the greater good—then the Y is the place for you. Join us!

Explore Openings

WE ARE...

Welcoming: We’re open to all. We create spaces where you can be, belong and become.
Genuine: We value who you are and encourage you to be true to yourself and others.
Nurturing: We’re with you on your journey to develop your full potential.
Hopeful: We believe in your ability to create positive change in our community and in the world.
Determined: We’re on a relentless quest to make our community stronger, beginning with you!

Love What You Do

When you work for the Y, you join an inclusive, welcoming, and fun environment where you can do meaningful work and make an impact in the community. The Y is a well-known, well-respected nonprofit like no other, and we truly care about our employees' personal growth, well-being, and advancement.
Apply
We offer a wide range of full-time, part-time, and seasonal jobs. Benefits vary based on your position but can include:
Free YMCA membership
Flexible scheduling
Generous discounts on Y programs, childcare, and camp
403b retirement plan, with exceptional employer contribution once vested
Ongoing career training and professional development opportunities
Health and dental insurance, life insurance, tuition reimbursement, and more for full time employees
Join a committed team who are passionate about strengthening community
Access to our employee assistance program (EAP)
Our core values are caring, honesty, respect, and responsibility—they guide everything we do.
Join us in the amazing work we get to do throughout the Newton area.
