About Us

At Sandmagination, we offer a one-of-a-kind indoor sandbox play experience that combines STEAM-based learning, sensory exploration, and hands-on creativity. Our interactive exhibits inspire children to experiment, build, and discover through open-ended, imaginative play. Whether you’re looking for a fun indoor kids’ attraction or an educational play space, Sandmagination is the place to be!

Sign Up Now

What We Do

We offer New England’s largest indoor sandbox, providing year-round opportunities for hands-on, sensory-rich play. Our focus is on fostering creativity, curiosity, and developmental growth in a space designed with families in mind.

Our Mission

At Sandmagination, our goal is to create a safe, fun, and enriching play environment for children aged 10 and under. As parents, we understand how important it is to have a space where children can learn and explore freely, while parents feel confident that their kids are in a safe and engaging space.

Join Our Community

Who We Are

We’re Katerina and Jason, a couple with children of our own, and we’ve built Sandmagination together because we believe every child deserves a space where they can thrive. With our own kids as inspiration, we’ve created an environment that blends fun with learning. Sandmagination is our shared vision for a place where families can come together, and children can explore, create, and grow. We’re dedicated to making this the best space not only for our kids, but for yours too.

When to come & where to park

Find our current hours, location, and parking details so you can plan a smooth, stress‑free arrival at Sandmagination.

Hours

7 Days per Week: 9:00 AM - 6:00PM

NOTE: On inclement weather days, tickets must be purchased in advance. Due to limited space, we cannot accommodate waiting inside or in the vestibule when we are at capacity.

We are generally open every Holiday. Check our Calendar for Holiday Hours

Location

Address: 210 Andover Street Suite P189 Peabody, MA 01960

IMPORTANT: Entrance is OUTSIDE the mall next to Macy's Men's

Parking

We are located at Northshore Mall with ample free parking available. During busy holiday periods, parking may be more limited, so we recommend arriving early.
