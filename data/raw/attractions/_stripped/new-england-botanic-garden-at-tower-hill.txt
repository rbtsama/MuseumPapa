Welcome to New England Botanic Garden at Tower Hill!

Experience the wonder of plants at a botanic garden in the heart of New England. We are a nonprofit organization open to the public year-round for garden viewing, trail walking, educational programs, exhibitions, and more. Our garden includes conservatories, formal and naturalistic gardens, a Garden Shop, café, and expansive views of the Wachusett Reservoir. We care for an irreplaceable collection of plants and place sustainability and environmental stewardship at the forefront of our work. Here, wandering is always welcome, and no two visits will ever be the same.

Privacy Overview

This Privacy Notice describes the personal information that New England Botanic Garden at Tower Hill (“ NEBG ”, “ we ”, “ our ”, or “ us ”) collects when you use the NEBG website at https://nebg.org/ (the “ Website ”), how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have in connection with this.

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND USE

Through your use of our Website, we collect personal information, which is information that identifies you as an individual or relates to you as an identifiable individual. We collect personal information from you directly and automatically as you use our Website.

A. Information You Provide To Us

We will collect personal information from you if you engage with our Website. Specifically, we collect personal information from you when you:

Donate . When you make a donation to NEBG, we will collect your identifiers (name, email address, title, organization name (if applicable), address, telephone number) and payment information (billing address, credit card number, CVV, expiration date). We will use this information to process your donation and provide any applicable tax form. Please note our payment processor collects and processes the payment information directly on our behalf and NEBG only maintains historical payment information.

Make a Purchase . When you make a purchase on our Website, we will collect your identifiers (name, email address, title, organization name (if applicable), address, telephone number) and payment information (billing address, credit card number, CVV, expiration date). We will use this information to process your purchase and communicate with you about your purchase. Please note our payment processor collects and processes the payment information directly on our behalf and NEBG only maintains historical payment information.

Subscribe to Our E-news . When you opt in to receive e-news updates, we will collect your identifiers (name, address and email address). We will use this information to email you our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link in each email. Please note that we will continue to send you notifications necessary to your membership or requested products or services. Our communications contain tracking technologies, provided by Constant Contact, to gain valuable insight into behavior of recipients. With the help of these technologies, we can analyze whether a predefined action took place by a recipient, such as opening our communications, in order to better adapt and distribute our communications. When you opt-in to our marketing communications, you are opting into the use of these technologies. You can disable tracking by disabling the display of images by default in your email program.

Enroll to be a Member . When you sign up to be a member, we will collect your identifiers (name, address, email address, telephone number), type of membership, or enrollment in a special membership program, and payment card information (card number, expiration date), and if you sign up online, your selected username and password. We will use this information to enroll you as a member, communicate with you regarding your membership, and process your membership payment. Your payment information will be securely shared with our payment processor, that will process the payment on our behalf. You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link in each email. Please note that we will continue to send you notifications necessary to your membership or requested products or services.

Plan to Make a Gift . When you request additional information about how to make a gift, we will collect, through our third-party gift management provider, your identifiers (name, email address, telephone number, address). We will also collect information about the type of gift you are inquiring about (e.g., by will or trust, life insurance, beneficiary designation, IRA charitable rollover), and whether you would like to be sent additional information regarding estate planning, scheduling a consultation, and illustration about the benefits you may receive by making a gift. We will use this information to process your request, and communication with you about your request.

Sign Up to Volunteer and Submit a Volunteer Timesheet . When you fill out our online form to sign up to volunteer, we will collect your identifiers (name, telephone number, email address), the date of your request, the area of NEBG with which you wish to volunteer, and any other information you wish to include in the “Messages/Comments” field. We will use this information to process your volunteer request and contact you about volunteer opportunities. When you submit a volunteer timesheet, we will collect you identifiers (name and email address), and the date number of hours and job you volunteered for. We will use this information to process the timesheet and share such timesheet with whomever you explicitly request us to share it with.

Inquire about a Private Event . When you inquire with NEBG through the Website about a private event (e.g., wedding, weekday meetings, social events, children’s parties) we will collect your identifiers (name, email address, membership status, telephone number, company name (if applicable), event details (type of event, description of the event, event date, event time, number of attendees), how you heard about us, and any other information you wish to provide. We will use this information to process your request to host an event at NEBG and communicate with you regarding your request.

Submit a Product to Our Online Shop . When you submit a request for NEBG to sell your products in our store, we will collect your identifiers (name, company name, email address, address), product/business website URL, product description, information contained in the files you attach to the request, retain price range, minimum order quantity, whether the product is handmade, whether the product is made locally or regionally, and other stores or websites where your product is sold.

Create an Account . When you create an account through our Website, we will collect your identifiers (email address and selected password). We will use this information to manage your account, facilitate tour reservations, and communicate with you about our services and offerings. If you have not opted-out of this when submitting your information, we use your name and email address to send you marketing communications. You may unsubscribe to promotional messages at any time by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of each email. If you unsubscribe, we will continue to send you messages related your account.

Contact us. When you send us a question or inquiry, you will provide us with your identifiers (name and email address) and any other information you choose to include in your correspondence. We use this personal information to respond to your questions or inquiries, troubleshoot where necessary, and address any issues you have with the Website or your interaction with NEBG.

Complete a Boxed Lunch Menu Form. When you complete a boxed lunch form, we will collect the from you your identifiers (name, email address), and details about your lunch menu request (number of each type of sandwich, date and time for pickup, tour visit date). We will use this information to process and fulfill (if possible) your request.

When you complete this form, we will collect from you, your identifiers (name, job title, business email address, business telephone number, business address) preferred method of contact, type of organization, interest in learning about opportunities at NEBG, how you learned about NEBG, and any additional information you wish to provide. We will use this information to process and facilitate your request and communicate with you about your request.

Complete a Sponsored Event Request Form. When you complete this form, we will collect from you, your identifiers (name, address, telephone number, email address), organization name, website, mission, organization EIN, type of event, date of event, number of attendees, description of the event. Complete a Group Visit/Tour Form. GENERAL ADMISSION DISCOUNTS

NEW ENGLAND BOTANIC GARDEN MEMBERS

Garden members, log in and reserve your free general admission tickets, or present your New England Botanic Garden member card at the desk when you arrive. To create an account, you will need to know your member number, if you need assistance finding your member number, please call at 508-869-6111 or email membership@nebg.org .

For guests that are covered by your membership level, please reserve member type tickets. Additional tickets for your guests may be reserved using the General Admission priced tickets. To use your complimentary Gate Passes for guests not covered by your membership, please reserve General Admission priced tickets and you will be prompted to apply your Gate Passes in your Cart.

FAQs

What are the dining options for groups?

Groups who are interested in enjoying lunch from our café, the Farmer and the Fork, must pre-order their boxed lunches at least one week prior to their arrival. Please note that orders must be made in one transaction and can be picked up in person between 11:30am-2:30pm. For more information, please contact Peppers Artful Events at eventinquiry@peppersartfulevents.com or call 508-393-6844.

Members of your group may also bring their own bagged lunch to eat picnic style in the gardens. No outside catering or alcohol is permitted. There are scattered tables throughout the gardens and in the Northeast Link (a small room on the east side of the Orangerie), and you may spread a blanket on any mown grassy area. Seating throughout the gardens is limited and available on a first come, first served basis. Café seating is reserved for customers of the café.

Garden staff are happy to provide a list of local restaurants should you decide to enjoy lunch elsewhere before or after you visit.

Are there cancelation and inclement weather policies for group visits?

Tours are held rain or shine. In the event of inclement weather, we will reschedule your tour, however, tour refunds are not available. Group rate admission fees still apply during New England Botanic Garden extended hours.

Tickets are non-refundable and only valid for the date and time listed above. Groups have one opportunity to choose to reschedule on their own based on availability for an additional fee of $50. Should a rescheduled date have different seasonal pricing, the group will be responsible for any increase in admission fees.

What is the best time to visit the Garden?

Any time! In spring enjoy warm weather and thousands of beautiful blooming bulbs including tender perennials daffodils, and tulips. In the summertime enjoy our collection of lilies, daylilies, vegetable garden, and more. The fall welcomes colorful New England foliage outside and vibrant conservatories inside. In the winter get out of the cold and enjoy our subtropical conservatories featuring citrus tree collections. In every season you can enjoy a plethora of seasonally interesting plants, trees, foliage and miles of walkable, wooded trails! Get a taste of what you will see in our  garden gallery .

Ticket Refund and Exchange Policy

Tickets for daytime, general admission are non-refundable and many only be redeemed on the date selected. If you cannot attend on the date for which you purchased tickets, you have several options:

You can reschedule your general admission visit to another currently available general admission date. Any difference in price will need to be paid at time of rescheduling. Please call us at 508-869-6111 or email visit@nebg.org no later than 4pm on the date on your tickets with a specific date for when you would like to reschedule.

You can give your tickets to another individual(s). Please note that individual tickets may only be redeemed for a single ticketed admission for equal or lesser value.

You may use a portion of the price paid for your ticket(s) as a credit towards a membership . We can only apply a credit for the admissions that would have been covered by the selected membership level. VISITOR GUIDELINES

Guidelines

To ensure you have the best visit possible, we encourage you to do the following:

Explore our website prior to your visit.

Click the maps below to download them, and learn about our unique garden spaces .

Check our hours of operations and note special announcements.

Dress for the weather and wear comfortable walking shoes. Bring water, a hat, sunscreen, and bug spray as needed. Insects, including ticks, bees, and mosquitoes, are part of our ecosystem.

Bring your own wheelchair, mobility scooter, stroller, or wagon. Wheelchairs are available to borrow on a first-come, first-served basis.

Visit the Garden Shop for gifts and tools for aspiring and veteran gardeners of all ages!

Add a membership to your ticket purchase for instant access to member discounted tickets! If you are a member, please bring your membership card to take advantage of discounts in the Garden Shop.

Follow us on social media, @newenglandbg, to learn about upcoming events.

Take photos! Photography Policy

Picnic on our lawns throughout the formal gardens.

Bring your dog to walk .

Please be considerate when using your cell phone.

Please refrain from the following during your visit:

Straying from the paths and disobeying posted signage and safety barricades.

Rearranging plants, plant labels, containers, benches and other structures that are part of the garden.

Sunbathing (shirts and shoes required at all times).

Climbing trees, picking plants and flowers, walking into fountains or water features.

Feeding and touching the wildlife.

Releasing plants, seeds, or creatures, including butterflies, at New England Botanic Garden at Tower Hill. To learn why this is important, please see Xerces Society butterfly release policy .

The following items are prohibited in the Garden:

Smoking, e-cigarettes, vaping, and any illegal substances.

Small group picnicking is allowed, but no barbecuing, outside alcohol, or outside catering. Carry out what you carry in, including trash.

Pets, except service animals and leashed dogs.

Toys that can cause damage to the plant collection and wildlife, such as balloons.

Radios, musical instruments, and equipment for active sports (balls, Frisbees, kites, scooters, and bikes).

Drones.

Firearms and other weapons.

Other Guidelines

Dog Guidelines

Leashed dogs are welcome year-round except during special after hours events, such as Night Lights. All dogs must be leashed, and are not permitted indoors further than the Visitors Center Lobby.

Dogs not covered by a Tails ‘n Trails membership are charged $8 admission per dog.

Only one dog per person is allowed to visit the Garden at one time.

Dogs are allowed to visit the Garden year-round, except during special after hours ticketed events such as Night Lights . Service dogs are welcome throughout the property during all open hours.

Dogs must be leashed at all times and under the control of their handlers. Dogs should not go off leash at any time during your visit.

The only area of the building where dogs are permitted is in the lobby of the Visitor Center at the Garden.

We ask that you please use discretion where you permit your dogs to relieve themselves. The Garden has a world renowned and scientifically important collection of plants, which may be sensitive to dogs’ urine. Dogs are not permitted to walk onto planting beds or into fields or woodlands. The Garden also has a high tick population so we encourage keeping your dogs on paths and trails.

Our ponds are habitats for birds, turtles, small fish as well as certain aquatic plants. In order to ensure these precious habitats remain intact, please do not allow your dog in or near the ponds. Even walking your dog on the edges of the ponds can damage this sensitive ecosystem.

Dog handlers are required to clean-up after their dogs. Dog handlers must use “doggie bags” and dispose of them in the designated receptacles.

The Garden has the right to revoke a Dog Membership or remove any dog at any time if dog handlers do not clean-up after their dogs or abide by any of the dog policies.

Aggressive dogs are not permitted. The Garden reserves the right to refuse entry to or remove any dog thought to be dangerous to guests or other dogs.

Tails ‘n Trails membership program and dog admission is only available for dogs. We ask that you leave all other pets at home.

Instructors Bringing Classes to NEBG

Instructors leading external for-fee classes must pay a $500 fee to visit the Garden with their class. In addition, the instructor and students must pay garden admission. All external fee-based class visits must be submitted in advance to the Private Events Department for approval by New England Botanic Garden at Tower Hill. Alternatively, you can contract with us to teach a program at New England Botanic Garden at Tower Hill. We work with instructors from across the United States to deliver high-quality education programming for adults. Find out more here.

Your payment information will be securely shared with our payment processor, that will process the payment on our behalf. You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link in each email. Please note that we will continue to send you notifications necessary to your membership or requested products or services.

Plan to Make a Gift . When you request additional information about how to make a gift, we will collect, through our third-party gift management provider, your identifiers (name, email address, telephone number, address). We will also collect information about the type of gift you are inquiring about (e.g., by will or trust, life insurance, beneficiary designation, IRA charitable rollover), and whether you would like to be sent additional information regarding estate planning, scheduling a consultation, and illustration about the benefits you may receive by making a gift. We will use this information to process your request, and communication with you about your request.

Sign Up to Volunteer and Submit a Volunteer Timesheet . When you fill out our online form to sign up to volunteer, we will collect your identifiers (name, telephone number, email address), the date of your request, the area of NEBG with which you wish to volunteer, and any other information you wish to include in the “Messages/Comments” field. We will use this information to process your volunteer request and contact you about volunteer opportunities. When you submit a volunteer timesheet, we will collect you identifiers (name and email address), and the date number of hours and job you volunteered for. We will use this information to process the timesheet and share such timesheet with whomever you explicitly request us to share it with.

Inquire about a Private Event . When you inquire with NEBG through the Website about a private event (e.g., wedding, weekday meetings, social events, children’s parties) we will collect your identifiers (name, email address, membership status, telephone number, company name (if applicable), event details (type of event, description of the event, event date, event time, number of attendees), how you heard about us, and any other information you wish to provide. We will use this information to process your request to host an event at NEBG and communicate with you regarding your request.

Submit a Product to Our Online Shop . When you submit a request for NEBG to sell your products in our store, we will collect your identifiers (name, company name, email address, address), product/business website URL, product description, information contained in the files you attach to the request, retain price range, minimum order quantity, whether the product is handmade, whether the product is made locally or regionally, and other stores or websites where your product is sold.

Create an Account . When you create an account through our Website, we will collect your identifiers (email address and selected password). We will use this information to manage your account, facilitate tour reservations, and communicate with you about our services and offerings. If you have not opted-out of this when submitting your information, we use your name and email address to send you marketing communications. You may unsubscribe to promotional messages at any time by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of each email. If you unsubscribe, we will continue to send you messages related your account.

Contact us. When you send us a question or inquiry, you will provide us with your identifiers (name and email address) and any other information you choose to include in your correspondence. We use this personal information to respond to your questions or inquiries, troubleshoot where necessary, and address any issues you have with the Website or your interaction with NEBG.

Complete a Boxed Lunch Menu Form. When you complete a boxed lunch form, we will collect the from you your identifiers (name, email address), and details about your lunch menu request (number of each type of sandwich, date and time for pickup, tour visit date). We will use this information to process and fulfill (if possible) your request.

Complete a Business Partnership Form. When you complete this form, we will collect from you, your identifiers (name, job title, business email address, business telephone number, business address) preferred method of contact, type of organization, interest in learning about opportunities at NEBG, how you learned about NEBG, and any additional information you wish to provide. We will use this information to process and facilitate your request and communicate with you about your request.

Complete a Sponsored Event Request Form. When you complete this form, we will collect from you, your identifiers (name, address, telephone number, email address), organization name, website, mission, organization EIN, type of event, date of event, number of attendees, description of the event. We will use this information to process and facilitate your request and communicate with you about your request.
