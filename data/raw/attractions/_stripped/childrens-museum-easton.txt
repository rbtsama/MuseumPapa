Location Address and Get Directions

9 Sullivan Avenue, North Easton, MA

Admission is $12 per person (Adults and Children 1 year and up). Advance ticket purchase is not required.

Hours:

Tuesday thru Friday: 9:30am to 3:00pm

Saturdays & Sundays: 12:30pm to 4:00pm

The last admission of the day is 45 minutes prior to our closing time as our doors lock at that time. Please plan accordingly.

All of our programs and activities are free with admission or a CME membership, while supplies last.

CME hosts school field trips on Thursdays during the month of May and June. We suggest you check our social media outlets for updates prior to visiting as visitation may be limited.

CME will be closed on Monday, Memorial Day.

Juneteenth Observance (June 19) CME is closed.

Hours & Admission Information

Tickets do not need to be purchased in advance.

HOURS:

Closed Monday

Tuesday through Friday 9:30am to 3:00 pm

Saturday and Sunday 12:30am to 4:00pm

Please plan for your visit accordingly as last entrance into the Museum is 45 minutes prior to closing time.

WEATHER RELATED CLOSURES - CME follows the policy of the Easton Public Schools for storm related closures. If the Easton Schools are closed, CME is closed. Check our social media accounts for updates.

ANNUAL SPRUCE UP - CME is closed each year for several days for our annual spruce up (begins September 1st). Please check our website and social media for details and dates as these vary from year to year.

HOLIDAY CLOSURES - CME will be closed on New Year's Day, Easter Sunday, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Columbus Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve and Christmas Day.

Admission Fee

$12 per person
Adults and Children 1 year and up

Members have unlimited FREE Visits.

Become a Member!

Planning to visit?

Our exhibits are currently designed for children ages 1 to 7 and some exhibits are designed for the older child as they could contain small parts. All children are welcomed but our current exhibits are best experienced for ages 2 to 7. Some mornings can be a bit busy with younger children so if you prefer a more quiet time, consider visiting after the noon hour.

Plan on a day filled with fun and exploration with your child! Here at CME we strive to ensure the safety of all of our visitors but is the sole responsibility of parents, grandparents and caregivers to monitor your child at all times.

For the health and safety of our visitors, shoes are a requirement for all children and adults.

Have you heard? The Wild Place, a fenced, attractive, educational and developmentally appropriate outdoor extension of the museum, is undergoing renovation. Spring 2026 will unveil a redesigned Wild Place with new age-appropriate equipment, enticing trails and areas to sit, relax and enjoy the outdoors!

Accessibility

CME is committed to maintaining an inclusive and welcoming environment for our visitors. The side door (handicap entrance) and lower level doors are locked for safety and security reasons. CME does not have an elevator at this time, but we do have ramps that have access to our Main Exhibit Hall as well as our MakerSpace and Birthday Party room. We will gladly assist you as needed.

Wheelchair Accessibility

A visitor using a wheelchair will be admitted free along with one child or companion. Wheelchair accessibility is located by the side door (to the right of the museum). CME does not have an elevator at this time but we do have ramps that access the Main Exhibit Hall and lower level where our MakerSpace Exhibit and Party rooms are located. Kindly call CME upon your arrival at 508-230-3789 and a staff person will open the side door to assist you.

Strollers

Strollers are best left outside. CME is a historical building with limited floor space and no elevator. When planning your visit, kindly consider bringing an on-the-body child carrier or backpack as they are a better option considering the stairs and building layout. If a stroller is necessary, please enter by the side door (by the handicap entrance) and a staff person will let you in.

Food and Drink

Food and drink are not permitted inside the Museum. It is our goal to keep the Museum safe for all visitors especially those with food allergies.

Partnerships

Card to Culture - EBT

EBT admission is offered for up to two immediate family members (residing in the same household) at $5.00 per person. A state issued EBT card must be presented to receive the discount. Discount is not valid during special events. Card to Culture is a collaboration between the Mass Cultural Council and the Executive Office of Health and Human Services' Department of Transitional Assistance . See the full list of participating organizations .

Blue Star Museum

We are a Blue Star Museum! We offer free admission for up to 6 immediate family members each year from Memorial Day to Labor Day. Discount is not valid during special events. Present an active or retired military i.d. to receive this discount. Discount not valid during special events.

MTA (Massachusetts Teacher Association)

Present your MTA card and you will receive free admission. Discount is not valid during special events.

Wonder Fund

Foster Care families or families with children who are in DCF Foster care, will receive 50% off admission for up to six family members when the Wonder Fund card is presented. Discount is not valid during special events.

Directions

The Museum is conveniently located near Routes 138, 24, 128, and 495.

GPS input should be 9 Sullivan Avenue, North Easton, MA.

Parking

Street parking is available on Sullivan Avenue along our Wild Place. Additional parking is available in the Town Lot behind the Museum.

Please help us be good neighbors and be sure to pay attention to street parking signs!

Visit the Children’s Museum In Easton

Come play and learn with us!

At the Children's Museum in Easton we can accommodate families and groups of all sizes. Your time with us will be full of learning, creativity and discovery and we've got all the info you need to plan a fabulous visit any day of the week.

View Hours • Admission Fees • Questions?

Exhibits

The Museum has three floors of interactive hands-on exhibits. Blast off into space, discover in the S.T.E.A.M. Lab, create in the MakerSpace and so much more awaits your imagination!

Current Exhibit Info

Educational Programs

Be sure you check out our daily educational programs that are free with admission! We've got science, music, art and literacy covered, all you have to do is come on in.

See Current Program Options

Group Visits/ School Field Trips

A visit to the Children's Museum will be an experience full of learning, creativity and discovery!

More Info on Group Visits

Parties at the Museum

Birthday parties at the Museum are fun and easy! You'll be assured that you and your guests will have a thoroughly good time. You can choose the party day and time that fits your needs Saturday or Sunday, 10 to 12 or 4 to 6.
