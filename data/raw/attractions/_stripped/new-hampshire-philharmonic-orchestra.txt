Folk Voices and Fantasies: Music Rooted in Culture and Imagination
May 30 | 31 @ 2pm

Guest Soloist: William Yeh,

2025 Sempre Musick Grand Prix Award Winner

www.SempreMusick.com

​

Igor Stravinsky: Petrushka

Max Bruch: Scottish Fantasy

Carlos Chávez: Sinfonía India

​

This concert celebrates folk traditions and cultural storytelling. Chávez’s Sinfonía India (1935–36) incorporates indigenous Mexican melodies and percussion, honoring native heritage with rhythmic vitality. Bruch’s Scottish Fantasy (1880) evokes the spirit of Scotland through folk tunes and Romantic lyricism, with the violin portraying a poetic narrator. Stravinsky’s Suite from Petrushka (1947) condenses his ballet about a tragic puppet into a vivid concert piece, blending Russian folk color, bold rhythms, and shifting moods. Together, these works offer a journey through national identity, folklore, and fantasy, revealing how composers reimagine tradition through bold orchestration and imaginative storytelling.

​

​ Auditioning for the New Hampshire Philharmonic

​

We are always seeking out talented musicians to join our ranks! To audition for the New Hampshire Philharmonic, please

Fill out the form to let us know of your interest, and we will get back in touch. ​

You will be asked to prepare a video submission of not more than 1-2 minutes of a fast/technical piece and not more than 1-2 minutes of a slower/lyrical piece, plus one scale of your choice ​

After review, we may invite you for an in-person audition, typically before the first Sunday rehearsal of a concert cycle.

You may find audition materials for certain instruments here at the link "Audition Materials." Sight reading may be involved. (We are working on adding all the instrumentation audition materials lists; we will work with you if your instrument's list is not yet prepared.)

NOTE: We are actively recruiting for violin, cello and percussion!

NH Phil Partners

Visit any of these participating local NH Phil partners and ask about a special discount code for 20% off, Senior and Adult, New Hampshire Philharmonic classical music concert tickets.

These codes are for online purchases only and cannot be used at the box office. There is no limit on number of tickets you can use with code.

​

If your organization would like to be this list, please reach out to us. Thank you to our Arts & Culture partners for participating.

Join our Partner Program

Want to become a Partner of the NH Phil? Send us a message and we'll be sure to add you to our list of participating partners. Send an email to toni@nhphil.org or call 603-647-6476.
