Admission Policy

Davis Farmland is a family-owned, Children’s Discovery Farm in Sterling, MA. We are a child-centered business and for the past 20 years we have focused exclusively on children who are age 12 years or younger (chronologically), and their accompanying adults. The safety of our guests is of paramount importance to us. We use child safe wristbands and a single entrance/exit security system. Davis Farmland is the first accessible farm in Massachusetts, taking all disabilities into account in our design. We have always strived to be at the forefront of security and comfort for our guests. Examples include a nursing room, multiple hand washing and sanitizing stations, diaper facilities, and family bathrooms.

Our Children’s Discovery Farm is an experiential learning environment for young children to learn by interacting with animals and playing in the great outdoors. We are home to North America’s largest sanctuary of endangered farm animals as well as having endless play events and structures that are specifically designed and developmentally appropriate for young children.

Any child age 12 years or younger is always warmly welcomed at Davis Farmland. Any adult who is accompanied by a child age 12 years or younger is also always welcomed. Over the years, we have received many requests for departure from this policy, but legally and logistically we are unable to make exceptions. Please understand that we have only two options: allow admission for everyone or restrict admission universally. As a Children’s Discovery Farm, we have chosen the latter in order to ensure the safety and comfort of our young guests.

In 2019, we began testing the concept of offering special events for all ages around our general Farmland schedule. We have since expanded this concept into multiple 21+ adult nights and All Ages Weekends on the farm.

Check our website often for the latest updated information.

Please Notice: NO outside alcohol, cook tops, grills, pets, smoking or vaping allowed at Davis Farmland.

Sheep Shearing Weekend
June 6 - June 7

Sheep Shearing Weekend Time to say goodbye to winter coats!

Kids can help the farmers give our sheep their summer hairdos and see all the fluffy fun up close!

It’s going to be SHEAR fun for the whole family!

Cowabunga Summer
June 19 - September 7

June 19-Labor Day
Splash & Laugh All Summer at The Cowabunga Splash Park!

The Cowabunga Splash Park at Davis Farmland is officially America’s largest zero-depth water splash park —and it’s bursting with fun for the whole family!

With two expansive spray pads —one for toddlers and one for multi-generational fun—parents and kids can play side by side, exploring every kind of mister, sprinkler, and geyser imaginable.

Chill under the Water Tower or Windmill , dash through the Rainbow Rings , splash through Bubble Blast , race down the Slip & Slide , and conquer our all-new inflatable waterslide !

Don’t miss the real firetruck and vintage seaplane straight from the Florida Keys —perfect for imaginative water play and photo ops!

Summer of Reptiles
June 19 - September 7

June 19-Labor Day 🐍🦎🐢

Get ready — Reptile Man, Michael Ralbovsky, is back and bringing the ultimate reptile adventure to Davis Farmland!

Meet snakes, lizards, and gators… explore the giant I-Spy Turtle Pond… and experience jaw-dropping, hands-on reptile shows every single day!

It’s wild, interactive, unforgettable — and included with your Davis Farmland admission!

June 21 - June 22

Kids—bring your dad for FREE on June 20th and 21st!

Make it a day full of snuggles, smiles, and unforgettable memories

Dads receive free admission—no need to reserve a ticket in advance. All other guests ages 2 years and older require an admission ticket for entry.

Faq’s and Policies

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you weather permitting?

Yes. We are open both weather and ground conditions permitting. Always call (978)422-MOOO(6666) or check the web before heading out.

What do I have to pay extra for?

Any food, drink, and merchandise purchases, as well as pony rides and animal feed cups are extra.

When does Cowabunga Splash Park open for the season?

Cowabunga Splash Park officially opens for the season on Father’s Day Weekend and closes Labor Day.

Does it get muddy when it rains?

It can, Davis Farmland is a real working farm! Our walking areas are all stone carved pathways, but puddles and mud do happen.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We are Cashless and accept most major credit cards. If you come to the farm with cash and not with credit, debit, or mobile, you will have the option to purchase a gift card. You can purchase a gift card at Guest Information. The gift card can then be used throughout the farm for purchases. If you have money left on your gift card the value that remains on the card can be used at our farm on future dates. It will not expire. Or you can go to Guest Services and receive cash back for any money left on the gift card.

Can I leave the farm and come back the same day?

You may leave and return to the farm as often as you like in the same operating day. You must retain your wristband for reentry. This includes going to your car for any reason.

Do you offer group rates?

We do offer group rates by advanced reservation only. Call 978-422-(MOOO)6666 for group requirements, availability, and reservations.

Do you offer stroller, wagon, or wheelchair rentals?

Yes, we have a limited number of strollers, wagons, and Wheelchairs available for rent on a first-come, first-serve basis. Due to the popularity of these rentals, we are unable to guarantee availability. Personal strollers, wagons and wheelchairs are always welcome.

Do I need to pay separate admissions?

Do I need to pay separate admissions for Davis Farmland, Davis Mega Maze, or U-pick apples and pumpkins? Yes. Our farmparks have separate admission and operating hours. Combo passes and combo group rates are available.

Can we bring our own food into the park?

Guests are welcome to bring a packed lunch. Complementary tables are provided on a first-come, first-serve basis. We ask that you not use tables for birthday parties and/or hold them for extended periods of time so that other guests may enjoy them too. Guest celebrating a special occasion may reserve a Party Package with reserved tables. Please properly dispose of all trash and clean-up your tables after eating. No alcohol, grills, or glass containers, please. Cooking on-site is strictly prohibited.

Can adults enter Davis Farmland without a child 12 years or younger?

Wristband Policy

Davis Farmland puts safety first. To ensure the safety of all guests who visit, there is one entrance and one single secure exit. At check-in, everyone in your group (including children under 2) will receive a wristband with a matching number. Please keep wristbands on the entire day. We will remove them at the exit matching up the number, making sure children are leaving with the correct adults.

If your child will be leaving with a different adult than they arrived with, both parties will need to go to Guest Information and we will adjust wristbands.

If a child in your group has lost their wristband, a photo will be taken at the exit of the adults leaving with the child and your photo ID. These photos are held securely and deleted every 30 days. If you do not have a photo ID with you, you will need to go Guest Information and complete the necessary forms to exit with that child.

These safety and security measures are designed to give you and your children peace of mind and comfort to explore, learn and play in a nurturing, secure environment.
