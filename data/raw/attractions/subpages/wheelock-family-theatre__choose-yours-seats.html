Seats for Every Family

With a range of pricing options, discounts, and ways to save, we make it easier for families to come together and share in the experience of live theatre.

Tickets & Pricing

We offer flexible ticketing options designed for families:

Seating Tiers

Choose your seats based on location and price preference.

  • Tier A – Premium seating
  • Tier B – Mid-range seating
  • Tier C – Value seating

Youth Pricing

Youth tickets (ages 3–18) are available across all tiers, making it easier for families to attend together.

All-Inclusive Pricing

The price you see is the price you pay—all ticket prices include fees, with no added costs at checkout.

Ways to Save

  • Season Memberships – Enjoy early access, flexible exchanges, and year-round savings
  • 3-Show Premium Package – Secure seats to all three productions with built-in savings
  • Community Programs – Including Card to Culture and group rates

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a ticket for my child?

Yes—everyone attending a performance, regardless of age, must have a ticket.

What is youth pricing?

Youth pricing is available for audiences ages 3–18 across all seating tiers, making it easier for families to attend together.

Are ticket prices inclusive of fees?

Yes. All ticket prices are all-inclusive, meaning the price you see is the final price—no additional fees at checkout.

How do I use a discount or promo code?

After clicking “Purchase Tickets,” you’ll be taken to the performance calendar to select your desired date and time. Once you reach the seating map, click “Have a promotion code?” in the upper right corner, enter your code, and select “Apply”—your ticket prices will update automatically.

If you need assistance at any point, our Box Office team is happy to help.

What discounts are available?

We offer a variety of discounts, including youth pricing, group rates, community programs like Card to Culture, and special offers for students, educators, and the Boston University community. Visit our pricing section above for full details.

Can I exchange my tickets?

Yes! Ticket exchanges are available based on availability. Season Members receive free exchanges as part of their benefits.

Do you offer refunds?

Tickets are generally non-refundable. If your plans change, we encourage you to exchange your tickets for another performance.

What if I purchased tickets through a third-party site?

Tickets purchased through third-party vendors cannot be refunded or exchanged through the Wheelock Family Theatre Box Office. Please contact the original seller directly.

Questions?

Send us an email at wfttix@bu.edu or call our box office, 617-353-3001.
