The Mother Church in Boston

Learn about the resources provided by The Mother Church in Boston 

The Mother Church and the Christian Science Plaza is open to all in the local Boston community. The church provides Sunday church services, Wednesday testimony meetings, Sunday School, a public Reading Room, and free talks on Christian Science. The Plaza also hosts the How Do You See the World? experience—including the MappariumⓇ globe—and events throughout the year.
