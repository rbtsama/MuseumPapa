Boston Family Days

Photo courtesy of the Boston Mayor’s Office—Jeremiah Robinson.

Photo courtesy of the Boston Mayor’s Office—Jeremiah Robinson.

Thanks to a partnership between the MFA and the City of Boston, Boston Family Days provide students who live in Boston and their families free access to the Museum on the first and second Sundays of each month. Learn more about how to participate, including how to register for the program.

The MFA offers a variety of special gallery activities on Boston Family Days. Check below for the full schedules by date. We look forward to welcoming you to the Museum!

Programs

Gallery Explorations

Discover art in different areas of the Museum with 30-minute themed tours and activities for all ages.

Drop-In Art Making

Art supplies are provided for these free art-making activities inspired by works in the collection.

Discovery Cart

Explore the materials and techniques artists use through discussion and demonstration.

Teen Sundays

Join us for a variety of activities geared toward teens, like tours, art making, and more.

UNIQLO Story Hour

Drop by for an interactive children’s story program.

Discovery Guides

Explore the MFA with in-gallery guides that are fun for all ages! Search for imagery, learn about the art, and get creative with drawing and writing prompts. Each activity focuses on a specific theme, linking objects across many time periods and cultures. Pick them up at Sharf Visitor Center on your next visit!
