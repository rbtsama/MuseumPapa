What’s Included with Admission

What’s Included with Admission
  • The J.K. Lilly III Automobile Gallery, featuring the From Carriage to Classic: How Automobiles Transformed America exhibit
  • The Special Exhibitions Gallery, featuring the Cranberries: From Cape Cod to American Icon exhibit
  • The American Art & Carousel Gallery, featuring the Heritage Highlights exhibit and rides on the 1908 Looff carousel
  • The gardens, featuring collections of rhododendrons, hydrangeas, daylilies, and more (seasonal)
  • Hidden Hollow- an outdoor discovery area for families
  • Remembering the Revolution exhibit, found throughout the galleries and grounds.
  • The Wampanoag Stories exhibit featuring a wetu, Three Sisters Garden, and mush8n
