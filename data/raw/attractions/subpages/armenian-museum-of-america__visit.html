Welcome

The Armenian Museum presents the largest and most diverse collection of Armenian objects outside of the Republic of Armenia. Our updated galleries include ancient Urartian artifacts, medieval manuscripts, Kütahya ceramics, textiles, liturgical objects, and contemporary art. Through an active special exhibition and events program, we provide new experiences for returning visitors as well as those who are new to Armenian culture and history.

We aspire to tell the story of the Armenian people through objects in the collection and to promote awareness and appreciation of the culture in engaging and dynamic ways.

Information

The following safety measures and guidelines have been implemented for your ease and comfort while touring the Museum:

• Commercial-grade HEPA Air Purifiers throughout all galleries
• On-site Security in Galleries
• Frequent professional cleanings
• Fully modernized elevator

All Museum galleries are wheelchair accessible.

Gift Shop

Be sure to visit and Buy Armenian in our Gift Shop! We’re stocked up on merchandise from Armenian vendors including Michael Aram, Anet’s Collection, and handmade items from the Homeland Development Initiative Foundation, a Fair-Trade Organization that provides income for women in villages throughout Armenia. Don’t forget, free admission and shop discounts of 10% to 25% apply for members!
