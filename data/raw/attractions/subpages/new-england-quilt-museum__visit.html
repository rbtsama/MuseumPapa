Visit NEQM

Hours

Tuesday through Saturday: 10 AM - 4 PM
Closed Sundays and Mondays

Admission

  • Regular Admission - $10

  • Children 12 and under - FREE

  • Museum Members - FREE  

  • EBT cardholders and 1 guest - FREE

Tours

Guided tours can be arranged for groups of eight or more guests.

Reservations are required no less than two weeks in advance. In addition to general admission, a fee of $30 will be charged for each docent required.

Contact the development office at giving@nequiltmuseum.org or 978-452-4207 ext. 18

Directions

By car:

Lowell is located 28 miles north of Boston and 22 miles south of Nashua.

From Interstate Route 495 take Exit 89C onto the Lowell Connector.

From Route 3 take Exit 80A if traveling southbound, Exit 80B if traveling northbound.

By train:

Commuter rail service is available by MBTA from Boston's North Station to Lowell's Gallagher Terminal.

Driving directions

Accessibility

The NEQM welcomes wheelchairs, personal walkers, and scooters. 

The entrance located on the left side of the Museum is wheelchair-accessible with a drop-off area and push button for staff assistance.  

On-street parking is free of charge for vehicles with a valid disability placard clearly displayed.

The second-floor galleries are accessible by elevator and feature benches.

A single-occupancy restroom is fully accessible for visitors who use wheelchairs or require assistance from a caregiver. 

Parking

Municipal Garages

Convenient nearby parking is available at the HCID Garage, Leo A. Roy Garage, Joseph Downes Garage and George Ayotte Parking Facility.

Metered street parking is also available. Vehicles displaying a valid handicap placard may park at meters for no charge.
