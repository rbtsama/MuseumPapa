Hours of Operation

Hours are subject to change; Check back to confirm before visiting!

April 1, 2026 – September 27, 2026

OPEN Wednesday – Sunday* | 9:30 am-5:00 pm

ALSO OPEN:

  • Monday, Monday, May 25 (Memorial Day)
  • Monday, Monday, September 7 (Labor Day)

*The Village will be CLOSED on Saturday, September 26, 2026 for our Revolutionary Gala

**Please note: Weekdays during the spring may be busy field trip days. Field trip groups usually visit the Village between 9:30 and 1:00, with the highest volume of field trips falling Wednesday – Friday between the end of April and mid-June.

September 30, 2026 – November 14, 2026

OPEN Wednesday – Sunday | 9:30 am-4:00 pm

ALSO OPEN: Monday, October 12 (Indigenous Peoples’ Day)

November 15 – November 26, 2026

CLOSED as we prepare for Christmas by Candlelight.

November 27 – December 27, 2026

OPEN select nights from 2:00 to 8:00 p.m. for Christmas by Candlelight. 

Mapguides

Upon arrival, guests will receive a guide that gives a summary of every building and a map of the campus. You can download PDF versions of the mapguides distributed January – November below. Programs like Phantoms by Firelight and Christmas by Candlelight have alternative mapguides. 

Visitors also receive a daily schedule upon arrival, outlining the day’s timed programs and other highlights. The daily schedule is also posted on our website approximately a week in advance and can be viewed here.

General Daytime Admission

Buy tickets here.

SAVE $3 per Adult, Senior, or Youth general daytime admission ticket by purchasing your tickets online instead of at the door!* Buy tickets online.

Daytime Admission Rates Online:

Adults $27.00
Seniors (60 and over) $25.00 
Youths (4-17) $12.00
Children (Age 3 and under) FREE

Daytime Admission Rates At the Door:
Adults $30.00
Seniors (60 and over) $28.00
Youths (4-17) $15.00
Children (Age 3 and under) FREE

*PLEASE NOTE: The $3 online discount cannot be combined with other discounts or applied to prior purchases. Standard Daytime Admission tickets are good for one day and are not valid for special programs like Christmas by Candlelight, Phantoms by Firelight, Fourth of July Celebration, or Redcoats & Rebels. If you have questions about purchasing daytime admission tickets please email [email protected] or call 508-347-0290.
