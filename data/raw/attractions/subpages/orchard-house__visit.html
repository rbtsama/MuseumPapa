Admission Rates & Discounts

We love our visitors, and hope you will become one soon, or, return for another visit!

Please take a few moments to browse our site for background on the Alcotts and Orchard House and to preview your visit experience.  Important details about touring Orchard House are provided below to assist with planning a visit, whether you're an individual, couple, family, school, scout troop, or group excursion.  If you need further assistance, please drop us a line via the Contact Form near the bottom of this page.  

Frequently asked questions

Parking for Orchard House

If parking spaces along the front of Orchard House are filled, additional parking is available in a large lot one short block away from us on Hawthorne Lane.  A crosswalk allows safe passage across Lexington Road.  Please do not park on Alcott Road or block neighbor driveways.  Thank you for your cooperation.  We look forward to seeing you soon!

Plan a Visit!

Orchard House is a "mask-friendly" site; face masks are optional for entry. To guarantee admission, we encourage reserving  Timed-Entry tickets online, but "walk-ins" may also be accommodated.  As always, our gardens and grounds remain free and accessible to all, while Virtual Tours provide an enjoyable way to experience Orchard House from the comfort of your own home.   

Reserve Timed-Entry


Other details about visiting Orchard House:


  • The House is shown by guided tour only; admission is charged
  • The visit experience lasts approximately 45 minutes
  • Tickets are primarily sold on a Timed-Entry reservation basis, but "walk-in" visitors (i.e., those who do not reserve tickets online) can often be accommodated as well
  • Timed-Entry Admission is non-refundable, but can be re-scheduled to a future date
  • Orchard House is handicapped-accessible on the first floor only; visitors with special needs or who are unable to climb stairs will be offered accommodations to enhance their tour experience
  • For reasons of security and visitor comfort, indoor photography, videography, eating/drinking, and active cell phone use are not permitted; your cooperation is appreciated
  • Organized groups of 10 or more persons (e.g., schools, motor coach tours, scouts, senior living facilities, book clubs, church youth groups) must make reservations by phone/email at least six weeks in advance (see further details below)
  • Please note that larger groupings of families or friends (10+ persons) can reserve Timed-Entry Admission online

timed-entry admission

Organized/Large Group Tour Reservations

  • Organized groups of more than 10 persons -- such as schools, motor coach tours, scouts, senior living facilities, book clubs, church youth groups -- must make reservations by phone/email at least six weeks in advance; please do not use Timed-Entry Admissions online for these types of larger groups
  • Organized groups are ideally accommodated on weekdays prior to or immediately at/after regular hours of operation (e.g., 9:30/10 am or 4:30/5 pm April - October and 10:30/11 am or 3:30/4 pm November - March);  other times may be available on a case-by-case basis (please email/call to discuss)
  • A $50 deposit is required to hold an organized group reservation and will be applied to the total tour cost
  • Cancellation of an organized group reservation must be made at least 48 hours in advance; deposits are only refunded if appropriate notice is received
  • To make an organized group reservation, please email groupreservations@louisamayalcott.org or phone 978.369.4118 x106
  • Please note that larger groupings of families or friends (10+ persons) can reserve Timed-Entry Admission online
