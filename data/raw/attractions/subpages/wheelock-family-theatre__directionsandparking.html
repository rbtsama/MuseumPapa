Directions & Parking

Get here with MBTA

Wheelock Family Theatre is a five-minute walk from both the Fenway or Longwood MBTA stops on the Green Riverside line (D train).

MBTA bus stops located on Brookline Avenue and in the Longwood/Hospital area are only a few short minutes away.

Driving Directions

Located in Boston’s Fenway neighborhood, Wheelock Family Theatre is easily accessible from Rt. 9, Rt. 2, and Storrow Drive.

The theatre is located on the Riverway, between the intersections of Brookline Ave and Longwood Ave.

Parking Information

Limited on-street parking is available in front of the theatre, please obey all posted signage.

Please note: “Live Parking” means that someone is sitting in the vehicle at all times while it is parked.

Wheelock Family Theatre patrons and guests can find parking at the MASCO garage located at 375 Longwood Avenue, between Brookline Avenue (Longwood Medical Area) and The Riverway. The garage is located behind Temple Israel and is only a short walk to the front doors of Wheelock Family Theatre. Please visit the MASCO website for more information on current discounted evening and weekend rates. There is no validation necessary for discounted parking at the MASCO garage.

Handicapped Parking
There are a limited number of handicapped designated parking spaces located on the Riverway in front of the theatre. Additional accessible parking can be found at the MASCO garage located at 375 Longwood Avenue. Please visit the MASCO website for more information.

Uber/Lyft/Ride Shares

Use our street address of 180 Riverway Boston 02215 to have your car service either drop you and your guests off at the theatre or for pick-ups.
