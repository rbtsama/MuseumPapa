Book Arts Open Studio

Our Book Arts Open Studio meets every second Saturday of the month from 10 am to 4 pm. Bring your projects and get creative among friends. Drop-in as you wish. There is a $10 studio fee, paid at admission, which gives you access to a variety of reference books & tools including: paper cutter, board sheer, book press and open conversation with other book artists.

HOURS

The Museum is open every Saturday, year-round, 10am to 4pm and on other days for special events. We open for groups and classes on request.

$15 general admission; $10 for children and seniors. Military, free. Free for Haverhill Library card holders thanks to a generous donation. No charge for members.
