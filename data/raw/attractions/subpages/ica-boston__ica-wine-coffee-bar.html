ICA Wine + Coffee Bar

The new waterfront ICA Wine + Coffee Bar offers light bites by The Catered Affair and a list of natural wines.

Hours

Thursday + Friday: 11 AM–8:30 PM
Saturday + Sunday: 11 AM–4:30 PM
Hours subject to change

The ICA Wine + Coffee Bar will close at 3 PM on ICA First Fridays.
