Pricing Information

2026-2028 Pricing:

Blithewold Manor, Gardens & Arboretum offers a refined and romantic setting for weddings of timeless elegance. Set on 33 acres overlooking Narragansett Bay, the historic estate features a grand manor house, sweeping lawns, century-old specimen trees, and seven meticulously maintained gardens in continuous bloom from May through October.

Couples enjoy exclusive use of the property for a single wedding per day, ensuring privacy, tranquility, and an unforgettable guest experience.

Blithewold offers a rare combination of historic grandeur, natural beauty, and thoughtful hospitality. The perfect setting for a truly memorable celebration.

The Setting

  • Great Lawn Wedding Tent: A 40’ x 100’ elegant tent with clear sides, permanent slate flooring, and specialty lighting, accommodating up to 180 guests. The tent is positioned on the 10-acre Great Lawn with sweeping bay views and spectacular sunsets.
  • Historic Manor Access: Exclusive use of the mansion’s first floor during the cocktail hour.

Wedding Ceremonies
Thirty-minute wedding ceremonies may be held in the picturesque Sunken Garden.

Sunken Garden Ceremony Fee: $2,500 which includes:

  • Ceremony rehearsal
  • Dedicated coordinator and staff
  • White garden chairs.

May – October
Reception rental fees apply to a five-hour event to be scheduled within the following time frames:
Friday & Saturday: 5:00 PM – 11:00 PM
Sunday–Thursday: 4:30 PM – 10:00 PM

Events may be extended by 30 minutes for $995 (Friday and Saturday only). Maximum event duration is six hours, inclusive of a 30-minute ceremony.

  • Fridays: $14,800
  • Saturdays/Holiday Sundays (May): $13,800
  • Saturdays/Holiday Sundays (June–October):$20,900
  • Non-Holiday Sundays & Weekdays: $10,500
  • Security Guard Required: $1,000

Unlike many venues, Blithewold is exempt from sales tax and does not charge administrative fees. At other venues, these fees can add $6,000 – $8,000 or more to your final cost.

What’s included:

  • Exclusive use of the manor’s first floor and 33-acre estate
  • Wedding tent with 20’ x 40’ dance floor and specialty lighting, accommodating up to 180 guests.
  • Event furnishings including:
    • 60-inch round tables
    • High and low cocktail tables
    • 8-foot banquet tables
    • Chestnut Willow chairs
  • Coordination of vendor deliveries and services
  • On-site Event Manager and Assistant throughout the event
  • Complimentary Bridal Suite available for five hours, beginning one hour priorto the event. Additional hour may be added for $500
  • One-year Blithewold membership

Guest counts exceeding 180 require a tent extension (estimated $2,500).

Catering & Additional Details

  • Exclusive Caterer: Blackstone Caterers (Five-Star Service), clambake and koshermenu options available upon request.
  • Catering is the responsibility of the lessee.
  • A 50% deposit of the reception fee is due at contract signing.
  • A $1,000 security deposit is required in addition to the mandatory securityguard fee.
  • Pricing and security charges are subject to change.
