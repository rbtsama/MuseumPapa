Hours / Admission Prices

Hours:

The Aviation Museum of N.H. is open to the public:

• Wednesdays through Saturdays 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.

• Sundays 1 to 4 p.m.

• Other times by appointment. Call (603) 669-4820 ext. 404 to arrange for a group tour or private visit.

Special April 2026 N.H. school vacation week hours and programs:

• The Aviation Museum will be open additional days during New Hampshire’s April school vacation week: Monday, April 27 and Tuesday, April 28 from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. each day.

• The Aviation Museum’s Flight Simulator will be running and open to the public on additional days during the New Hampshire February school vacation week:

  • Monday, April 27 from 1 to 4 p.m.
  • Wednesday, April 29 from 1 to 4 p.m.
  • Friday, May 1 from 1 to 4 p.m.

• Story time for younger visitors will be offered on the following days during the upcoming N.H. February school vacation week:

  • Tuesday, April 28 at 10:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m.
  • Thursday, April 30 at 10:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m.

Admission prices:

Effective Wednesday, Jan. 14, 2026, the following admission prices will be in effect at the Aviation Museum of N.H:

• Adults 13 and above, $15

• Youth age 6 to 12, $7.50

• Children 5 and under, FREE!

• Seniors (65+), veterans, active military, $7.50

• Aviation Museum members, FREE!

• Library Pass: Maximum of four people on one pass; additional visitors in party must pay appropriate admission fee.

• ‘Museums for All ‘discount: $5 for all members of a family (up to four people total) presenting a valid federal EBT card.

The Museum is staffed by volunteers of the New Hampshire Aviation Historical Society and all admission fees and donations support exhibits, education programs, operations and maintenance.
