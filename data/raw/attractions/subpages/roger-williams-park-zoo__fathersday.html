Celebrate Father’s Day at the Zoo – Free Admission for Dads!

Calling all animal lovers! This Father’s Day, treat Dad to a WILD adventure he won’t forget. Celebrate all the amazing fathers out there with a day full of fun, family, and fascinating wildlife. Dads, grandpas, and great-grandfathers enjoy free Zoo admission!

Explore the wonders of the animal kingdom together and meet some of the Zoo’s incredible animal dads. You’ll discover just how wild and wonderful fatherhood can be.
