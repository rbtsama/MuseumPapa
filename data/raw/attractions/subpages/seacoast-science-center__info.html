Info Night: Canadian Maritimes Eco-Adventure

  • March 4, 2025
  • 6:00pm – 7:00pm
  • Seacoast Science Center Get Directions
    • Eco-Adventure
About the trip Learn More

Join us to learn about our 2025 Eco-Adventure to Canada’s Maritime provinces.

Join SSC and friends on an eco-adventure to explore Canada’s coastal wonders, from the breathtaking wilderness of Cape Breton to the charming  shores of PEI. You’ll enjoy exclusive experiences including private conservation talks, gourmet seafood feasts, and behind-the-scenes cultural interactions.
