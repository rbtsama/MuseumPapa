location + parking

The Danforth Art Museum at Framingham State University is located at 14 Vernon St. on the Framingham Centre Common. Free parking and main entrance are at the rear of the building, which is accessed by Grove Street, located to the right of the Danforth Building. The lot entrance is the first left off Grove Street. The Museum is on the second floor and the art school is on the third floor.

Mailing address: 100 State Street, P.O. Box 9101, Framingham, MA 01701. Do not use “14 Vernon Street,” mail will be returned as undeliverable.

GPS Note: Enter “14 Vernon Street, Framingham” (not Danforth Art Museum or Art School) for accurate GPS navigation.
