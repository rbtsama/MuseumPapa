LAWN EVENTS
‚Äã
Join us nearly every Saturday and Sunday from May to October for our vibrant Lawn Event season. Embracing a rich tapestry of marques and eras, our events showcase a splendid array of Italian, German, British, American, Custom, Tuner, Alternative Fuels, Trucks, Hot Rods, and European Motorcycles. Each weekend brings a unique display, ensuring a friendly atmosphere and a celebration of the diverse world of automobiles. We can't wait to share the joy of these gatherings with you!
To register your show vehicle, visit the Lawn Event page. While not all events offer online registration, you can register your vehicle on the day of the event. Keep in mind that online registration does not guarantee a space on the lawn, so arriving early is recommended, even if you registered online.
‚Äã
If you're joining us as a spectator, just drop by at any time during the show, check in at the front gate for a wristband, and you're good to go. Tickets never sell out. Your admission to the Lawn Event also grants you access to the Museum.
‚Äã
Please be aware that, on occasion, events may conclude early due to weather or unforeseen circumstances. Additionally, it's important to note that Larz Anderson Auto Museum is not liable for theft, damage, or any valuables left in vehicles on the premises during Museum Events at Larz Anderson Park. We look forward to your participation and hope you enjoy the event!

TRAILERING A VEHICLE
‚Äã
If you are trailering a vehicle to a Lawn Event, please be advised there is absolutely NO trailer parking within the grounds of Larz Anderson Park. You MUST park your trailer outside of the park grounds. Limited parking spots within the park are limited to Park Visitors and Museum Guests. Unauthorized trailers will be asked to move via Museum PA or DJ and then towed
There is NO overnight parking available.
You may unload your trailer within the park and then move to Newton Street. When entering the grounds, please bear right and check in with a Museum staff person before unloading.
For more information or questions about lawn events, please call the Lawn Event Manager at 617-522-6547 x13 or by email at lawnevents@larzanderson.org.
