MAY 29 - JUNE 28. 2026
ANNIE
Leapin’ lizards! Everyone’s favorite family musical comes to GBSC for the first time!
Annie
is the heartwarming, Tony Award-winning classic that follows the adventures of a spirited young orphan determined to find her parents. Set in 1930s New York City, Annie’s journey takes her from the strict orphanage run by the cruel Miss Hannigan to the lavish home of billionaire Oliver Warbucks. With the help of her dog Sandy and a little “Tomorrow” optimism, Annie discovers the true meaning of family in this beloved musical filled with unforgettable songs and big-hearted charm.

Something Rotten! Jr
.
24-HOUR MUSICAL
Saturday, May 2nd at 7 pm.
Travel back to the Renaissance for an hour of musical comedy gold in
Something Rotten! JR.
In the 1590s, brothers Nick and Nigel Bottom struggle to write a hit play in Shakespeare’s shadow. When a soothsayer predicts the future of theatre is musicals, they set out to write the first-ever musical. On opening night, they discover that success means staying true to yourself and all that jazz.
