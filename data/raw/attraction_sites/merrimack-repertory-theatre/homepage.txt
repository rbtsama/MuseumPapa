Merrimack Repertory Theatre (MRT) builds community through theatre. Our shows celebrate new stories, new interpretations of classics, new voices on stage, new audiences, and new collaborations that help people feel, think, and connect.
Learn more about MRT
Plan Your Visit
MRT is committed to delivering an exceptional theatre experience for every guest, bringing a dynamic mix of drama, music, comedy, and powerful cultural narratives to the stage. Learn how to get here, where to park, and discover great dining options in the area.
Plan Your Visit
Education & Engagement
MRT offers engaging educational opportunities, including discounted student matinees with enrichment materials and special rates for educators. Budding young performers can participate in summer theatre programs that build skills and confidence on the stage.
Learn More
Support the Theatre
Help sustain live theatre in Lowell by contributing to Merrimack Repertory Theatre. Your support fuels new stories, community programs, and education initiatives that connect and inspire. Give in the way that works best for you—through annual contributions, sponsorships, matching gifts, or planned giving.
