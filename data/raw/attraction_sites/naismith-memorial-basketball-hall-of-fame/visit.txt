Discover the birthplace of basketball
The Naismith Basketball Hall of Fame is a fantastic destination for everyone from lifelong basketball fans to families with children who are just beginning to explore the world's most popular sport. The Hall of Fame is located in "The Birthplace of Basketball", beautiful Springfield, Massachusetts. The city is easily accessible no matter what direction you are traveling from.
